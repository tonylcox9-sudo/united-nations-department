The Crisis

When the Healer Cannot Heal Themselves

Modern medicine demands everything — long shifts, emotional labor, administrative overload — and offers little in return. The result is a global epidemic of physician burnout, early retirement, and tragic outcomes.

  • Burnout rates exceed 50% in every major medical specialty.
  • Vacation guilt keeps doctors at work even when their bodies beg for rest.
  • No coverage systems mean taking time off burdens colleagues.
  • Administrative punishment is real for those who prioritize wellness.
"I haven't had more than three consecutive days off in four years. I don't remember what rest feels like."
— Emergency Physician, Syria
"We are expected to be superhuman. But we are human. And we are breaking."
— Surgical Resident, Chicago
What We Do

Three Pillars of the Department

01

Coverage Networks

We build vetted locum and cross-coverage networks so your patients are safe and your colleagues are not burdened when you rest.

02

Protected Scheduling

We partner with hospitals and clinics to embed mandatory, non-negotiable vacation blocks into physician contracts.

03

Advocacy & Research

We publish data, lobby institutions, and shift culture so that taking vacation is seen as professional strength, not weakness.
